Games

Frog and Croc

Frog and Croc is a 3D retro platformer that I made with a team for my Game Development class. The team consisted of three programmers and one artist. I was in charge of gameplay programming, level design, UI, sound programming, and voice acting. This game was inspired by 3D platformers and collectathons from the late 1990's and the early 2000's.

Reflector VR

This game was made as a final project for my Interactive Virtual Environments class. I worked with a team consisting of four artists and two programmers on a VR game that we named Reflector VR. I worked on gameplay programming, level design, and AI behavior.

Dungeon in the Dark

I developed this game for my Intro to Video Game Level Design course. I was the only developer on this project, but I was able to use environment assets, gameplay code, music, and sound effects from Unity's Asset Store and the YouTube audio library. Other than a few code snippets from the Asset Store, I was responsible for all of the programming as well as the level design, set dressing, and lighting.

Honey Up! - First Place Winner

In Chillennium 2019, I formed a team with three of my friends - two artists and one other programmer. The theme for this game jam was "one for all, and all for one". I worked on gameplay programming, AI navigation, and UI. Honey Up! is a a real-time-strategy game that was developed in 48 hours.

I am incredibly happy to announce that this game won First Place Overall out of about 90 games submitted.

Block Builder: City Escape - Award Finalist

In Chillennium 2018, I was in a group with four programmers. I worked on gameplay programming, level design, and animation programming. The theme for this game jam was "transformation". The game we created was a 2D puzzle-platformer made in 48 hours.

This game was a Chillennium Award Finalist.

Summon the Spellbook

This game is currently in development, and has made incremental progress since the summer of 2019. I have been working with three of my friends to design this game and implement mechanics. This particular level of the game as made for my Level Design class. All of the programming and level design was done by me, and all of the art assets and music were received from free online sources.

I plan to continue development with my team in the near future.
