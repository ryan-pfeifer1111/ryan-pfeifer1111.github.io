About

I am a Computer Science student at Texas A&M University, graduating in May 2020. I have a double minor in Game Design and Development as well as Cybersecurity. When I first started coding in high school, I was fascinated by programming. I started making small games and other software on my own time to try and understand how some popular applications might work. I am inherently curious and I like to discover solutions to problems through a learn-by-doing approach.

Throughout college, I have been introduced to many different programming languages, but I am most comfortable with C++ and Java. I also have significant experience in several other languages such as C#, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python.

By taking different courses in my major and enrolling in clubs and organizations, I've found that I have more of an affinity for creating visual applications. I spend a lot of my free time creating and playing games, learning about new technologies in computer graphics, and making websites.

There's a lot more that I'm eager to learn about, and I can't wait to contribute my skills to something great!
