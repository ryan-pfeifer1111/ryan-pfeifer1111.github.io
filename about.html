About

Hey there! I'm Ryan Pfeifer, and I'm a Game Developer from Texas A&M University. During my time in college, I majored in Computer Science and obtained a minor in Game Design and Development.

Since graduating college, I have been working at ArtCraft Entertainment as a Gameplay Programmer to help ship an MMORPG called Crowfall, as well as an upcoming unannounced project. By working on projects both professionally and recreationally, I have gained significant experience designing and implementing a multitude of game mechanics and systems across a wide range of genres and team sizes.

After working as a Gameplay Programmer for almost 2 years, I've realized that I have a creative itch for Design that I've been unable to scratch in my current position. As a result, I am now looking for new opportunities that will afford me the creative freedom that I crave, with a particular interest in Technical Design and Level Design positions.

Don't be a stranger! I'd love to chat with you about openings for these roles. Feel free to reach out via LinkedIn or email. There's lot that I'm eager to learn about, and I can't wait to contribute my skills to something great!
